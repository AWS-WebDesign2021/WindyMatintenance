Simple Embellished Ring


Overview

This lovley simple embellished ring is from Pandora. It is a thin sliver band with small cubic zirconia stones spaced evenly across it. This ring is a classic and would work well for anyone, it is great for stacking and the stones shine greatly for their quality. Some small scratches have appeared but they are not noticable from a normal distance.

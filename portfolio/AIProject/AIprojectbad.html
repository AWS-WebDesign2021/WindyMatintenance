AI Enabled Weaponry




Home The World Bee Project AI Enabled Weaponry Resources
Artificial intelligence is becoming far more common in many fields, but an unlikely one that has a significant moral impact is its rising use in the military. Autonomous and AI enabled weapons are at the center of this discussion, which many countries are actively developing without a clear understanding of what this means for the future. China, the United States, and other nations have fully functional autonomous systems that can define a target and lock onto it. Currently there is a human required to oversee all lethal semi-automated weapons, they require a person “in-the-loop,” but as these weapons are developing quickly and being sold around the world without clear rules, who knows how long lethal automated weapons will require a human to make such a terminal desiscon. The true concern with all of this is the lack of clear guidelines, as this is such new technology. Conferences and discussions have happened, but no laws have been defined, different nations have created different definitions of what an AI weapon means, letting them each decide which weapons are allowed and which are not. Who knows what steps a desperate nation will take during, “The fog of war.” As Professor Peter Asaro said, “It's important to realize that targeting a weapon is an act -- a moral act, choosing to pull the trigger, to engage that weapon, is another moral act. These are two crucial acts that we should not have become fully autonomous.”

About This Webpage

This web page was made to bring awareness to a complex ethical issue in tech, my web page was about artificial intelligence. It includes two different case studies and applications of AI and an overview of the topic. This web page was my first time working with a partner, which was an important learning experience for me in collaboration. It was also my first time using animations and transitions, there are two pictures that move back and forth, a bouncing title, and a transition that changes the color of a title. Looking back I think I could have been more intentional with my animations so that they feel more relevant to the topic, I also think I could have given it more organized formatting. This is also one of the more sophisticated pages that I have made, but the biggest impact that it made for me was working with another person, and learning about an important ethical issues.

Pavé Hearts Ring


Overview

This beautiful pavéd ring is from Pandora. The entire outide of the ring is pavéd with small cubic zirconia stones and shines gorgeously. The band itself has small heart cut-outs that give the ring a cute unique look. One issue with these cut-outs is that they require cleaning more than many other rings. Overall this band is great on its own or stacked and is a perfect afordable pavéd option.


Place in Collection

This ring holds a special place in my collection as it was the first one I recived and was a graduation gift. It has held up perfectly over the time that I have had it. I wear it on my left middle finger stacked under the simple embellished ring.

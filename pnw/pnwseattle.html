Our urban experience

Seattle is a gorgeous city and the biggest in the Pacific Northwest, it has a great skyline and great activities for tourists and natives to enjoy. It is situated between the Pacific Ocean, Cascade Mountains, and Lake Washington, making it a perfect spot to relax and enjoy the view. We offer multiple different activities meant to let you explore the city, and also wind down and relax.

Ride the Great Wheel

Take a ride on the impressive Great Wheel in downtown Seattle, the largest wheel of this type on the West Coast. Set right on the water near many other shops and activities, this Ferris wheel is an amazing luxury gondola experience that give on to views of the Space Needle, and the Puget Sound. At night the wheel lights up with over 500,000 LED lights. The wheel is known to shine in colors of sports events, holidays, and every color of the rainbow. Your ride will be private, comfortable, and inside an inclosed heated gondola. This activity is great for anyone getting to know the area and who wants an affordable night to remember.
